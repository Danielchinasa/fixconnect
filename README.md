**Connecting skilled artisans with customers, seamlessly.**

FixConnect is an open-source, two-sided marketplace mobile app (iOS & Android) designed to bridge the gap between local craftspeople and individuals seeking reliable home services.

## âœ¨ Features

- **Dual Onboarding:** Separate flows for Customers and Artisans.
- **Smart Search & Filtering:** Find artisans by category, location, ratings, and price.
- **Secure Booking & Escrow Payments:** Book services and pay securely with funds held in escrow until job completion.
- **In-App Messaging:** Direct communication between users.
- **Ratings & Reviews:** Build trust within the community.
- **Admin Dashboard:** For platform management and dispute resolution.
