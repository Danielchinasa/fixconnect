# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to make participation in this project a positive, enjoyable, and respectful experience for all contributors.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Being open to feedback and constructive discussion
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using inclusive and welcoming language
- Taking responsibility for your actions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, insulting or demeaning comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Excessive or aggressive argumentation
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional workplace

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying acceptable behavior
- Taking corrective action when inappropriate behavior occurs
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Banning contributors for repeated or severe violations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Project documentation
- Discussions, Slack/Discord groups, or related platforms
- Any public space where project work is done

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Warning

Clear explanation of violation and expectations for improvement.

### 2. Temporary Ban

Contributor is blocked for a limited period.

### 3. Permanent Ban

For repeated or extreme violations.
